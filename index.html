What is Permanent Dentition?

Ano ang Permanenteng Ngipin?

Permanent dentition refers to the second set of teeth that replace primary (baby) teeth. There are 32 permanent teeth in total, composed of:

Ang permanenteng ngipin ay ang pangalawang hanay ng ngipin na pumapalit sa primarya (baby) teeth. May kabuuang 32 permanent teeth, binubuo ng:

  • 8 Incisors (cutting food)
  • 4 Canines (tearing food)
  • 8 Premolars (crushing/tearing)
  • 12 Molars including 4 third molars or wisdom teeth (grinding food)
  • 8 Incisors (pamputol ng pagkain)
  • 4 Canines (pampunit ng pagkain)
  • 8 Premolars (pampiga/pampunit)
  • 12 Molars kasama ang 4 third molars o wisdom teeth (pang-giling ng pagkain)

These teeth are meant to last a lifetime and play essential roles in chewing, speaking, facial structure, and overall oral health.

Ang mga ngiping ito ay panghabambuhay at mahalaga sa pagnguya, pagsasalita, estruktura ng mukha, at pangkalahatang kalusugan ng bibig.
